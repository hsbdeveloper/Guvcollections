About us

Our Story

Established November 2019, Guv's Collection is the new sister company to the wildly popular and long running Lucky Fashions. Guv's collection is quickly building a reputation as being one of the most popular haberdashery stores in Yorkshire. Ranging from rare fabrics to the perfect finishing touches on any bridal dress Guv's aims to please. With the philosophy the customer is always right, we always strive to provide the best possible customer service. No matter what the need pay Guv's Collection a visit and if its not in store we will do our best to get you what you need.
